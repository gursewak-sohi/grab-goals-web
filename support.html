Support for GrabGoals

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using GrabGoals.

  • How do I reset my password? - You can reset your password from the login page by tapping on "Forgot Password?"
  • How do I create a new tournament? - Navigate to the Tournaments section and click on "Plus Icon" or "Create Tournament."

Troubleshooting Tips

Experiencing issues? Try these steps:

  • Ensure your app is updated to the latest version.
  • Restart the app and try again.
